Who We Are

The Art of the Perfect Cut

The Gentleman's Cut was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every man deserves to look and feel his absolute best. Founded in 2010 by master barber Jonathan Cross, our shop has grown from a single chair to one of New York's most celebrated grooming destinations.

We combine the timeless traditions of classic barbering — the hot towel, the straight razor, the meticulous detail work — with the freshness and versatility demanded by today's modern gentleman. Every visit is a ritual, every cut a masterpiece.

Our Mission

Elevating the Standard of Men's Grooming

Our mission is to provide an unparalleled grooming experience that honors the craft of barbering while embracing modern style. We believe in creating a welcoming space where every gentleman feels valued, respected, and leaves looking his very best.

We invest in ongoing education for our barbers, sourcing only the finest tools and premium products to ensure that every service we deliver is nothing short of exceptional.
